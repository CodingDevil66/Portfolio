About Me,
I'm Kaushik Aryan, a passionate Software Engineer and Data Scientist based in Patna, Bihar. With a keen eye for languages like Python, Java, and some Apache tools like Hadoop and Spark, I am dedicated to bringing creative ideas to life and delivering meaningful experiences.

My Journey,
I am currently a Final year student at Haldia Institute of Technology pursuing B.Tech in CSE-Data SCience Field.I have done training on big data handling using Hadoop and Spark. I have certification in Python language. These experiences and trainings have not only honed my skills but have also ignited my passion for Data Science Field.

My Approach,
I approach every project with divide and conquer method. I also pay attention to small details to the projects which can become a dealbreaker. I believe that beauty lies in simplicity and I like to keep things simple and clean. Whether it's crafting an intuitive user interface, designing captivating visuals, or writing elegant code, I strive for excellence in every aspect of my work.

What I Love,
When I'm not immersed in computers and codes, you can find me with a canvas drawing up my imagination or with uniquely portable magic known as books.
